UPPSC Staff Nurse Admit Card 2017
Name of Post: UPPSC Male /Female Staff Nurse Admit Card 2017
Post Date: 06 December 2017 | 08:50 PM
Short Info: Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission, UPPSC Allahabad are Recently Uploaded Admit Card For the Recruitment Post of Staff Nurse Male/Female Post Examination 2017 . Those Candidates Who have Applied for this Recruitment Exam Can Download Admit Card.

Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission,  UPPSC

Staff Nurse Male/Female Recruitment 2017 Admit Card

Advt No. A-1/E-1/2017

Important Dates

  • Application Online Start:  12 January 2017
  • Last Date for Registration: 16 March 2017
  • Last Date Fee Payment : 16 March 2017
  • Last Date Final Submit Form : 20 March 2017
  • Exam Date : December 2017
  • Admit Card Available: 06 December 2017

Application Fee

  • General/ OBC : Rs. 125/-
  • SC/ ST Candidates : Rs. 65/-
  • PH  Candidates : Rs. 25/-

Payment Mode

  • Pay Exam Fee Through SBI E Challan Fee Mode.
  • Debit Card/ Credit Card / Net Banking Fee Mode

Age Limit on 01/07/2017

  • Minimum Age : 21 Years
  • Maximum Age:  40 Years 
  • Age Relaxation Extra as Per Rules.

Vacancy Details

Male: 448 | Female: 3390 |  Total Vacancy : 3838 Post

Eligibility for 3390 Female Candidates

  • Passed Intermediate Exam in Science Stream From Recognized Board .
  • Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery or B.Sc Degree in Nursing with Certificate as a Nurse and Midwife.

Eligibility for 448 Male Candidates

  • Passed Intermediate Exam in Science Stream From Recognized Board
  • Diploma in General Nursing and psychiatry or B.Sc Degree in Nursing with Certificate as a Nurse and psychiatry.

How to Download Admit Card

  • Step 1 : Click on "Download Admit Card" Link Given Below Side.
  • Step 2 : Enter Your Registration No. and Date of Birth (DOB).
  • Step 3 : Enter Gender.
  • Step 4 : Enter Verification Code.
  • Step 5 : Click on Download Admit Card Button.
  • Step 6 :View Status of Admit Card and Print out it.

Interested Candidates Can Read the Full Notification Before Apply Online Form.
