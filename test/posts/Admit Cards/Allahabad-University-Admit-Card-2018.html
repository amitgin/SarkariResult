Name of Post: Allahabad University All Course Admit Card 2017
Post Date: 03 December 2017 | 11:00 AM
Short Info: Allahabad Central University Are Recently Uploaded the Admit Card for the Annual Examination of Under Graduate Courses BA, B.Com, B.Sc. 2016. All Those Applicants Who Are Enrolled with Allahabad University for UG Courses or It's Affiliated College Can Download The Admit Card for this Annual Exam.

Allahabad University, AU

Under Graduate Course Annual Examination Admit Card 2017

Important Dates

  • UG Courses BA. B.Com, B.Sc Exam Start on : Feb to March 2018
  • Admit Card Available : 03 December 2017
  • Result Available : May/June 2018

Admit Card Available For

  • BA First, Second and Final Year Annual Examination 2016
  • B.Sc First , Second and Final Year Annual Exam 2016
  • B.Com First , Second and Final Year Annual Exam 2016
  • Other Courses Admit Card Will be Available Soon

How to Download Admit Card

  • Go to the Important Links Section and Click on download Admit Card Link.
  • Fill Your Enrollenment No. and Select Your Course.
  • Enter Verification Code (Capcha) and Click on "Submit" to Download or Print admit Card
