Short Info: Council Of Scientific  and Industrial Research (CSIR) Are Recently Uploaded Admit Card for The JRF and NET (Lecturer) December Examination 2017. Those Candidates Who have Applied for this Recruitment Exam Can Download Admit Card.
 

Council Of Scientific  and Industrial Research (CSIR)

Joint CSR UGC Test for JRF & NET December 2017 Admit Card

Important Dates

  • Online Application Start : 23 Aug 17
  • Last Date For Apply Online : 15 Sep 17
  • Last Date For Complete Form : 16 Sep 17
  • Last Date For Receipt Hard Copy : 23 Sep 17
  • Exam Date : 17 Dec 17
  • Admit Card Available : 09 Dec 17

Application Fee

  • General Candidates : Rs. 1000/-
  • OBC Candidates : Rs. 500/-
  • SC & ST Candidates : Rs. 250/-
  • PH Candidates : Rs. 250/-

Payment Mode

  • Pay the Examination Fee Through E Challan at any Indian Bank Branch.

Age Limit on 01/07/2017

  • For JRF : Maximum Age 28 Year.
  • For Lecturer / NET : No Age Limit

Eligibility

  • Candidates have M.Sc Degree and Equivalent with 55% Marks For General / OBC Candidates For SC / ST Candidates 50% Marks.
  • Integrated Course and B.E / B.Tech / B.Pharma and MBBS Candidates Are also Available for CSIR NET 2017.

Subject for Test

  • Chemical Science, Earth, Atmospheric, Ocean and Planetary Science.
  • Life Science, Mathematics Science, Physical Science.

Send Application Form To

  • The Deputy Secretary (Exam), Human Resource Development Group, Examination Unit, CSIR Complex, Library Avenue, Pusa, New Delhi - 110012

How to Download Admit Card

  • Step 1 : Click on "Download Admit Card" Link Given Below Side.
  • Step 2 : Enter Your Form No. and Date of Birth (DOB).
  • Step 2 : Enter Security Code.
  • Step 3 : Click on Login Button.
  • Step 4 :View Status of Admit Card and Print out it.

Interested Candidates Can Read the Full Notification Before Apply Online.
