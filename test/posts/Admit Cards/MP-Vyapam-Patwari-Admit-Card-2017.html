Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPPEB)

MP Vyapam Patwari Recruitment 2017

Important Dates

  • Online Application Start : 28 October 2017
  • Registration Last Date: 15 November 2017
  • Fee Payment Last Date : 15 November 2017
  • Correction Last Date : 16 November 2017
  • Exam Date : 09-31 December 2017
  • Admit Card : 02 December 2017

Application Fee

  • General & Other State Candidates : Rs. 570/-
  • OBC / SC & ST Candidates : Rs. 320/-

Payment Mode

  • Pay the Examination Fee Through Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / MP Online Authorised KIOSK  Fee Mode Only.

Age Limit on 01/01/2017

  • Minimum Age : 18 Years.
  • Maximum Age : 40 Years.
  • Age Relaxation Extra as Per Rules.

Eligibility

  • Candidates Have Bachelor Degree in any Stream with MP CPCT Exam Passed.

Exam Center

  • Jabalpur, Indore, Bhopal, Ujjain, Gwalior, Neemach, Mandasaur, Ratlam, Guna, Khandwa, Satna, Sagar, Seedhi, Katni, Damoh.
