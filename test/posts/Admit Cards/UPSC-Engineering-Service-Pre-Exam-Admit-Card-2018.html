Short Info: Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Are Recently Uploaded Pre Exam Admit Card for The Recruitment Post of Engineering Service Examination 2018. Those Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering Candidates Who have Applied for thie Recruitment Exam Can Download Admit Card now for Pre Exam.

Union Public Service Commission (UPSC)

Important Dates

  • Online Application Start : 27 September 2017
  • Last Date For Apply Online : 23 October 2017 upto 06:00 PM
  • Last Date For Fee Payment : 23 October 2017
  • Last Date For Fee Payment (Offline) : 22 October 2017
  • Prelim Exam Date : 07 January 2018
  • Admit Card Available : 11 December 2017

Application Fee

  • SC / ST Candidates : Rs. 0/-
  • PH / Female Candidates : Rs. 0/-

Payment Mode

  • Pay the Examination Fee Through Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / E Challan Fee Mode.

  • Minimum Age : 21 Years.
  • Maximum Age : 30 Years.
  • Age Relaxation Extra as Per Rules.

Eligibility

  • Candidates Passed / Appearing Degree in Engineering From Recognized Board / University in India.
