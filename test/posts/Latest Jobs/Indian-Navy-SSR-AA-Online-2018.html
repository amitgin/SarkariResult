  • Last Date Complete Form : 10 December 2017

Application Fee

  • General and OBC : 0/-
  • SC and ST: 0/-
  • No Application Fee for the All Candidates Only Fill the Online Form

Vacancy Details

  • Post Name : Senior Secondary Recruits (SSR)
  • Education Eligibility : Passed the 10+2 (Intermediate) Exam with Mathematics, Physics and One of the Following Subject : Chemistry / Biology / Computer Science
  • Physical Eligibility : Height : 157 CMs
  • Age Limitation : Candidates must Born Between 01/08/1997 to 31/07/2001
  • Post Name : Artificer Apprentice (AA)
  • Education Eligibility : Passed the 10+2 (Intermediate) Exam  with Mathematics, Physics and One of the Following Subject : Chemistry / Biology / Computer Science with At Least 60% Marks
  • Physical Eligibility : Height : 157 CMs, Running 1.6 Km in 7 Minutes And 20 Squat Ups
  • Age Limitation : Candidates must Born Between 01/08/1997 to 31/07/2001

Instruction for Photo

  • Candidate Need to Upload Good Quality Passport Size Photo with Blue Background
