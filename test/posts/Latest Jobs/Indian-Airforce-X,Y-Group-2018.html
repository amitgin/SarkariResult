X Group | Y Group Airmen Recruitment 2018

  • Admit Card Available : February 2018

Application Fee

Payment Mode

  • Pay Examination Fee Through Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / Axis Bank E Challan Fee Mode.

Age Limit

  • For X & Y Group Candidates : Age Between 13/01/1998 to 02/01/2002
  • Kindly Read the Notification For More Details.

X Group (Technical) Eligibility Details

  • Candidates Passed 10+2 (Intermediate) Exam with Math, Physics, English with 50% Marks OR
  • 3 Years Polytechnic Diploma with 50% Marks.

Y Group Eligibility Details

  • Candidates Passed 10+2 (Intermediate) Exam with 50% Marks, English 50% Marks OR
  • 2 Years Vocation Course with 50% Marks.

Physical Eligibility Details

  • Height : 152.5 CM.
  • Chest : Minimum Expand : 05 CM.
  • Minimum Weight : 55 KG.
