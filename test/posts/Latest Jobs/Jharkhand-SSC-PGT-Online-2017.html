Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission, JSSC

Post Graduate Teacher PGT Recruitment 2017

Advt No. 10/2017 PGTTCE

Important Dates

  • Online Application Start : 01 December 2017
  • Last Date For Apply Online : 30 December 2017
  • Last Date For Pay Exam Fee : 01 January 2018
  • Last Date For Complete Form : 08 January 2018
  • Exam Date : February 2018
  • Admit Card Available : January 2018

Application Fee

  • General & OBC Candidates : Rs. 600/-
  • SC & ST Candidates : Rs. 150/-
  • PH Candidates : Rs. 150/-

Payment Mode

  • Pay the Examination Fee Through Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / E Challan Fee Mode.

Age Limit as on 01/01/2017

  • Minimum Age : 21 Years.
  • Maximum Age : 40 Years.
  • Age Relaxation Extra as Per Rules.

Eligibility

  • Post Graduate Degree in Related Subject with 50% Marks for SC / ST 45% Marks with B.Ed Exam Passed.
  • For Experienced Candidates : Post Graduate Degree in Related Subject with 50% Marks for SC / ST 45% Marks with B.Ed Exam Passed and 3 Years Teacher Experience.
